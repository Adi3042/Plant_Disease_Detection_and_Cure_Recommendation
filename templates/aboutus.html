About Us

Welcome to the Plant Disease Detection System, an innovative AI-powered platform designed to help farmers and agriculturists identify plant diseases quickly and accurately. Our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture by reducing crop losses and improving productivity.

About Our Project

Our project, Plant Disease Detection System, is an AI-powered solution designed to identify plant leaf diseases with exceptional accuracy. Leveraging deep learning techniques and a large annotated dataset, our model achieved a remarkable training accuracy of 99.32%.

The main goal of our system is to assist farmers and agriculturists by providing quick, reliable, and automated diagnosis of plant diseases, reducing crop loss and supporting sustainable agriculture.

This project was developed as a part of our academic initiative. It was mentored by Dr. Snehlata, whose expert guidance was instrumental throughout the development. Our team includes dedicated members: Aditya Yadav (Project Lead & ML Developer) and Adnan Riyaz (Research & Deployment).

We combined state-of-the-art transfer learning models, data preprocessing techniques, and intuitive UI to ensure both high performance and usability. The system is also integrated with web and mobile platforms for easy access.

Our Vision & Innovation

The Plant Disease Detection System uses advanced deep learning to identify crop leaf diseases with exceptional precision. Designed to assist farmers and agricultural experts, our solution provides real-time insights using just an image of the infected leaf.

By harnessing the power of transfer learning and a robust image dataset, we achieved a model training accuracy of 99.32%. This ensures reliable predictions, making it a practical tool for field use and academic research alike.
