About Us

Welcome to the Plant Disease Detection System, an innovative AI-powered platform designed to help farmers and agriculturists identify plant diseases quickly and accurately. Our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture by reducing crop losses and improving productivity.

About Our Project

The Plant Disease Detection System is an AI-powered application developed to accurately identify plant leaf diseases. Using deep learning and the publicly available PlantVillage dataset, our CNN-based model achieved a remarkable testing accuracy of 99.39%.

The goal of this project is to support farmers and agronomists by providing fast, reliable, and automated disease diagnosis, thereby reducing crop losses and promoting sustainable agriculture practices.

This system integrates transfer learning, class balancing, and an intuitive user interface. It is accessible through both web and mobile platforms, making it a scalable solution for real-world use.

The project was developed as part of an academic initiative under the mentorship of Dr. Snehlata. Our team consists of Aditya Yadav (Project Lead & ML Developer) and Adnan Riyaz (Frontend Developer).

Our Vision & Innovation

The Plant Disease Detection System uses advanced deep learning to identify crop leaf diseases with exceptional precision. Designed to assist farmers and agricultural experts, our solution provides real-time insights using just an image of the infected leaf.

By harnessing the power of transfer learning and a robust image dataset, we achieved a model testing accuracy of 99.32%. This ensures reliable predictions, making it a practical tool for field use and academic research alike.
