Apple Scab

🦠 What the disease is:
Apple scab is caused by the fungus *Venturia inaequalis*. It creates dark, scabby lesions on leaves and fruits.

💊 How to cure it:
Use fungicides such as Captan or Mancozeb, especially in early spring.

🛡️ Prevention methods:
Plant resistant varieties, prune trees to improve airflow, and remove fallen leaves.

🌿 Ayurvedic/natural remedies:
Neem oil spray weekly in early spring.

💊 English Medicine/pesticides:
Captan 50WP, Sulphur-based fungicides.

Corn Rust

🦠 What the disease is:
Corn rust is caused by the fungus *Puccinia sorghi*. It appears as orange or brown pustules on leaves.

💊 How to cure it:
Apply fungicides like Propiconazole or Tebuconazole.

🛡️ Prevention methods:
Plant resistant hybrids and rotate crops to reduce fungal spores.

🌿 Ayurvedic/natural remedies:
Spray a mixture of garlic extract and water on affected leaves.

💊 English Medicine/pesticides:
Propiconazole-based fungicides.

Tomato Leaf Mold

🦠 What the disease is:
Tomato leaf mold is caused by the fungus *Passalora fulva*. It causes yellow spots on leaves that turn brown over time.

💊 How to cure it:
Use fungicides like Chlorothalonil or Copper-based sprays.

🛡️ Prevention methods:
Ensure good ventilation in greenhouses and avoid overhead watering.

🌿 Ayurvedic/natural remedies:
Spray diluted neem oil on affected plants.

💊 English Medicine/pesticides:
Copper oxychloride, Chlorothalonil.
