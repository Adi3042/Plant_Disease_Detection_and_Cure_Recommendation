What We Collect

At PlantDisease Detector, we believe in minimal data collection while providing maximum value. Here's what we gather:

  • Account Information: Only when you choose to create an account (name, email, and encrypted password)
  • Plant Images: Uploaded solely for disease detection, automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve our services (never tied to personal identity)
  • Contact Information: Only when you reach out to our support team

We use MongoDB Atlas with enterprise-grade security to store this information securely.

How We Use Your Data

Every byte of data we collect serves a specific purpose in enhancing your experience:

  • Plant Health Analysis: Your uploaded images power our 99.37% accurate AI detection system
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous usage patterns help us optimize performance
  • Customer Support: To personally respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Research: Aggregated, anonymized data contributes to agricultural science (opt-out available)
Data Protection

We implement military-grade security measures to safeguard your information:

  • Encryption: All data in transit and at rest using AES-256
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access for our team
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments every quarter
  • Data Minimization: We only collect what we absolutely need
  • Automatic Deletion: Plant images purged after 30 days, analysis results after 90 days

Our security protocols exceed GDPR requirements and are certified under ISO 27001 standards.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Ask us to erase your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Opt-out of specific processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at aditya523523@gmail.com.

  • Improvements to our security practices
