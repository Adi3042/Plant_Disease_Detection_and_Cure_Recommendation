Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Plant Disease Detection is an AI-powered platform that helps identify plant diseases from images of leaves or other plant parts. Our system analyzes the images and provides information about potential diseases and treatment options.

Our system has an accuracy rate of approximately 95% for common plant diseases. Accuracy may vary depending on image quality and the specific disease.

Account & Usage

No, you can use the basic detection service without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your history and access additional features.

Free accounts can analyze up to 10 images per day. Premium subscribers have unlimited access.

Technical Questions

We accept JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats. For best results, use high-quality images with good lighting and focus on the affected plant parts.

Analysis typically takes 5-10 seconds per image, depending on server load and image complexity.
