Plant Disease Detection Using Deep Learning

We have submitted our research paper titled "Plant Disease Detection Using CNN and Keras Tuner: A Deep Learning-Based Approach" to the IEEE INDISCON 2025 (6th International Conference).

This paper presents our end-to-end methodology using the PlantVillage dataset, a CNN-based model enhanced with Keras Tuner, and achieved a testing accuracy of 99.39%. It emphasizes early disease detection, practical field applicability, and sustainability in agriculture.

The paper is currently under review, and we are looking forward to its acceptance and presentation at the conference.
